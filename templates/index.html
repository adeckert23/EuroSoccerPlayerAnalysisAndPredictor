Premier League Attacking Player of the Year

Alex Deckert April 17th, 2019 33 Comments

While the player of the year debate centers on Raheem Sterling and Virgil Van Dijk, because their teams are the two frontrunners for the premier league this year, we feel not enough attention is being paid to what Eden Hazard is doing on the field this year. With the highest amount of dribbles and assists in the league this year, he is clearly unplayable. He also leads the league in the number of times he is fouled per game, so it looks like other teams have found a consensus on how they want to slow him down, whether they are doing it, is another story.

Jadon Sancho - Tearing up the Bundesliga at 19

Alex Deckert April 16th 2019 14 Comments

At 18 years old and not getting playing time for Manchester City, Jadon Sancho made the leap to the Bundesliga, to a team that had a history of developing and playing young players. After his first year, we can say that this experiment has been a success for both parties. Although a high level of mis-controlled balls which we believe will be harnessed over time, it has also lead to the highest amount of dribbles/game in the league, as well as the highest assists in the league. Plentry more good things to come, from this future (or current) superstar.
