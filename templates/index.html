Jadon Sancho - Tearing up the Bundesliga at 19

Alex Deckert April 16th 2019 14 Comments

At 18 years old and not getting playing time for Man City, Jadon Sancho made the leap to the Borussia Dortmund, in the Bundesliga, to a team that had a history of developing young players. After his first year, we can say that this experiment has been a success for both parties. He leads the league in dribbles/game, as well as the highest assists in the league. Plentry more good things to come, from this future (or current) superstar.
